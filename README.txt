ConnectLife brings your air conditioners into Homey. Switch them on or off, set the temperature and mode, adjust the fan speed and swing, and read the room temperature, straight from Homey or from your Flows.

Sign in once with your ConnectLife account and your units are added automatically. Because the ConnectLife cloud only reports when asked, Homey refreshes a unit right after you control it, and an optional background refresh keeps everything in sync with changes made from the remote or the ConnectLife app.
